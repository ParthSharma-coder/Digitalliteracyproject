TASK- 4 : Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide

Part B – Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:

Do’s :
* Share content that reflects positively on your academic and professional identity.
* Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts.
* Verify information before reposting or sharing.
* Engage respectfully in discussions and debates.
* Balance your online presence with offline responsibilities.

Don’ts :
* Post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
* Overshare personal details like addresses or financial information.
* Spread rumors, misinformation, or unverified news.
* Use social media during class or study time excessively.
* Engage in cyberbullying or negative online behavior.
